Network and infrastructure engineers covering switching, routing, firewall management, and SD-WAN. Our engineers hold certifications from Cisco (CCNA–CCIE), Juniper (JNCP), Palo Alto, Fortinet, and Aruba. Suitable for enterprise refreshes, MPLS migrations, and NOC monitoring roles.

Deskside & Field Support

Experienced IMAC (Install, Move, Add, Change) engineers and VIP support technicians deployed directly to end-user environments. Our field engineers handle hardware refreshes, device migrations, imaging, Active Directory management, and executive tech support across corporate campuses.

  • IMAC & Break/Fix
  • VIP & Executive Support
  • Hardware Rollouts & Refresh

Data Centre Operations

Smart Hands and Remote Hands specialists for colocation and on-premise data centres. Our DC technicians perform rack-and-stack, structured cabling (copper & fibre to Cat6A, OM3/OM4), hardware installation and decommission, KVM/OOB management, and 24/7 NOC-to-floor liaison. Trained to BICSI and Tier-3 DC standards.

  • Smart Hands & Remote Hands
  • Rack/Stack, Cabling, KVM
  • 24/7 DC Technician Placement
